Factor 1: Profile Completeness

Not all data fields are equal. We assign weights to your 12 fields based on their utility for sales contact and qualification. Email and Phone are critical; Employee Count is secondary.

Logic: Sum of filled weighted fields = Raw Profile Score (0-100).

Factor 2: Lead Intent

Once communication starts, behavior trumps demographics. We categorize your intents into four tiers, assigning an "Intent Value" to each action. Positive Closure actions drive the score highest.

Logic: The system takes the highest observed intent score recorded for the lead.
